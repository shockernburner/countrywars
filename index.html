Last carefully reviewed: 22 June 2026. “No opponent listed” means none appears under this project’s conservative rules. It does not prove a country has never experienced violence.
How the list was madeBase data uses the Correlates of War interstate-war dataset for 1816–2007. A conservative update layer adds clearly documented direct fighting between state forces after 2007. Civil wars, militant groups, piracy, threats, and most small border incidents are excluded. Palestine–Israel is included with a special caution because statehood, occupation, and armed-actor status are historically and legally complex.
